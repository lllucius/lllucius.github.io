KAOS is a small system that is primarily designed to manage the startup of guests within the z/VM environment after an IPL. It utilizes IBM's Operations Manager for its message and timer triggering.

It may also be used as an extension to Operations Manager to provide conditional execution of the various Operations Manager commands.

For more information, view the readme.
